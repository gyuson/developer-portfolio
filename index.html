About Me

I’m a developer, project manager, and problem solver who started building for the web since COVID. I focus on developing websites and applications for small businesses, helping you get the word out there.

More About Me

I took the non-traditional route to development. Working in finance and strategy for 12+ years doesn’t give you a lot of time for anything else, but it did hone my problem solving and project management abilities. COVID hit the world in 2020, time stood still, and I finally had the space to teach myself to code (and signed up for a bootcamp or 2).

In quarantine, I got to spent virtual time with a few friends who were running their own small businesses that range from food to retail to services. Jointly with them, I realize that a good number of small businesses would benefit from having better online presence in order to accelerate sales and growth.

These days, I run my web dev side hustle remotely from the beautiful town of Örebro, Sweden. I look forward to building your next website or application.
